Who We Are

Daret is a sustainability-driven startup focused on simplifying recycling and promoting a circular economy in Egypt. We believe that waste is simply a resource in the wrong place, and we're here to change that.

Our Mission

To make recycling accessible, rewarding, and impactful for everyone. We aim to create systems that make doing the right thing the easy thing.

Our Vision

A future where waste is no longer waste, but a valuable resource, and a real wealth. We envision a cleaner Egypt powered by community action and smart technology.
